Nigeria's Energy Gap

A Transformative Moment for Energy Access

With over 85 million people lacking reliable electricity, Nigeria represents one of the largest energy access opportunities globally. The market for off-grid and mini-grid solutions is growing rapidly as government policy, development finance, and private capital align.

Rural and peri-urban communities, along with millions of micro, small, and medium enterprises, are ready for clean, reliable, and affordable power — creating significant demand for distributed solutions like those Meridian develops.

Government & DFI-Backed Electrification

National electrification programmes backed by the Nigerian government and development finance institutions are channelling hundreds of millions of dollars into mini-grid and solar system deployment — creating a transformative opportunity for experienced private developers like Meridian Renewables to deliver energy access at scale.
