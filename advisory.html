What We Deliver

Four integrated advisory capabilities spanning the full investment lifecycle in African distributed energy

01

Technical Due Diligence

Independent engineering review of asset designs, EPC contracts, yield forecasts, and O&M frameworks for lenders and acquirers.

  • Generation yield modelling and validation
  • EPC contract and procurement review
  • O&M framework assessment
  • Technology risk evaluation
02

Financial & Transaction Structuring

Complex financial modelling, PPA negotiation support, and capital stack optimization for large-scale DRE portfolios.

  • Project and portfolio financial models
  • PPA and tariff negotiation support
  • Capital stack and blended finance design
  • Investor-ready documentation
03

Regulatory & Policy Navigation

Strategic counsel on permitting, tariff approvals, and regulatory compliance within evolving West African energy frameworks.

  • Permitting and licensing strategy
  • Tariff approval and compliance
  • Regulatory risk assessment
  • Policy landscape monitoring
04

Market Entry & Strategy

Pipeline origination, competitor analysis, and macroeconomic risk assessment for new capital entering the SSA distributed energy market.

  • Market sizing and opportunity mapping
  • Competitive landscape analysis
  • Macroeconomic and FX risk profiling
  • Pipeline origination and deal flow
