Pillar 1

Mini-Grids & Mesh Grids

Our solar hybrid mini-grids combine utility-grade solar PV arrays with lithium-ion battery storage, smart distribution networks, and digital metering to deliver reliable 24/7 electricity to communities that need it most.

Our mesh-grid architecture enables rapid, modular deployment with significantly lower connection costs — scalable to serve growing communities including households, schools, clinics, and SMEs.
